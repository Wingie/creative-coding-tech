Session 2: AQAL for Planning üîÆ

Four Quadrants of Effective Projects

UL - Interior Individual

I (Subjective)

  • My intentions & motivations
  • Energy levels & capacity
  • Inner resistance or excitement
  • "Why does this matter to me?"
UR - Exterior Individual

It (Objective)

  • Concrete tasks & deliverables
  • Deadlines & milestones
  • Tools & systems used
  • "What needs to be done?"
LL - Interior Collective

We (Intersubjective)

  • Cultural narratives about productivity
  • Team values & shared meanings
  • Inherited "shoulds" from family/culture
  • "What stories shape this?"
LR - Exterior Collective

Its (Interobjective)

  • Organizational structures
  • Economic systems & constraints
  • Tech platforms & infrastructure
  • "What systems enable this?"
